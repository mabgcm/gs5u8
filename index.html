Project Title:
Ancient Civilizations

Make a poster about your favorite ancient civilization.

Join a team to do the project!

You can make your poster individually but doing the project in a team would make the things easier and be much more fun!

  • Make a team of 4, 5 or 6 people.
  • Brainstorm the content of your project.
  • Share the tasks and responsibilities with your team members.
  • Collect the information and prepare the materials you will need for the project. You may need to make a research to find important details and pictures.
  • Don't forget: The project will be paper based. You will need the materials like scissors, glue, color pens etc.

You can start the project now!

The Civilizations

Elements of Civilizations

You can give different kinds of information about your favorite ancient civilization. Your areas of interest about the civilizations can be some or all of the areas below.

Architecture

What type of homes, temples, castels and palaces did they build in different civilizations? You can find pictures on internet or you can draw them.

Economy

How did the people in ancient civilizations trade? Did they use money or gold? Maybe they had different ways of trading in ancient times.

Food and Agriculture

What did the people in ancient civilizations eat? Did they grow vegetables or did they hunt the animals? How did they cook their food?

Lifestyle

How was the life like in ancient times? How did people celebrate their festivals? What did they do when they were happy or sad? Did they go to school?

Location

Where on Earth was your favorite civiization? What things and where did the archologists find related with this civilization?

Language

What language did they speak in ancient times? Is their language still speaking in today's world? Did they know how to write? Did the archeologist find something written at that time?

Presentation

The most important part of the project is PRESENTATION! Your speaking skills will be graded during your presentation. To get higher grades, you should follow the followin points during your presentation:

  • Speak confidently.
  • Look at your audience during the presentation. Make eye contact!
  • Be careful about the pronunciation of the words.
  • Be sure that your sentences are grammatically correct.
  • Speak slowly and loudly enough.
